Michael Study & Discover

Student exchange travel agency

Used skills:
  • WordPress
  • HTML5
  • PHP
  • SCSS
  • CSS3

Michael Study & Discover was a student exchange travel agency that sadly had gone out of business. However while operating they were offering students from Unite Kingdom and opportunity to study abroad in countries such as Australia, Canada and New Zealand.

Their aim was to create a website showcasing wonderful possibilities that each and every one of those countries proposed for the students. My task was to present large loads of text in attractive form, accessible to young people with not much time on their hands.
Website also contained a fully functional contact form for further communication between the applicants and company. I have set the second level of access for users that would set up an account on the website and another one for those who will decide to pay agency fee (also via the website).

The website was fully developed and functional for about 2 years while the company was operating. It is mobile friendly.
