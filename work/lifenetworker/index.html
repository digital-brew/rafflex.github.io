Life Networker

Courses on successful B2B networking

Used skills:
  • PHP
  • WordPress
  • SCSS
  • CSS3
  • HTML5

Life Networker is a company located in Manchester, United Kingdom providing courses on successful B2B networking.

The goal for the website was mostly to create a blog and raising personal profile of company founder, Wojciech Kolodziejczak.
When it comes to functionality, beside blog, I have created a subpage with Expert’s Panel that linked directly to interview and personal profiles of Experts. There is also a subpage providing users with opportunity to sing up for Life Networker’s workshops.
The website is fully developed and mobile friendly.
